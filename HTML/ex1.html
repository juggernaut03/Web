The Best Movie According to Me

The Top 3 Movies

Rockstar (2011):

Directed by Imtiaz Ali, "Rockstar" is a romantic musical drama starring Ranbir Kapoor and Nargis Fakhri. The film follows the journey of Janardhan Jakhar, a young man from Delhi who dreams of becoming a rockstar. He undergoes a transformation into the rebellious and passionate musician Jordan, whose tumultuous love story with Heer (Nargis Fakhri) shapes his career and personal life. "Rockstar" explores themes of love, pain, and artistic expression, accompanied by a soulful soundtrack composed by A.R. Rahman.

Tamasha (2015):

Directed by Imtiaz Ali, "Tamasha" is a romantic drama featuring Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone in lead roles. The film revolves around Ved and Tara, two free-spirited individuals who meet in Corsica and decide to spend time together, pretending to be different characters. However, their brief encounter leaves a lasting impact on both of them, leading to a journey of self-discovery and introspection. "Tamasha" delves into themes of societal expectations, conformity, and the pursuit of personal identity, underscored by soul-stirring performances and a captivating narrative.
